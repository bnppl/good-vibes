# Agentic Verification: TDD, Comprehension Debt, and Governance

Professional AI-assisted development in 2026 is defined by a shift from **code generation** to **code verification**. As AI agents become more capable of generating massive volumes of code, the primary bottleneck—and the primary risk—is the human ability to verify, understand, and govern that output.
